Access Information

Please note that access to the ChapterOS dashboard requires explicit permissions. By default, new accounts are created with a 'Visitor' role, which has restricted access and will result in a 403 Forbidden error on most pages.

After creating an account, your request will be automatically sent to the Chapter Chair and Webmaster for verification. Access will be granted once security checks are passed and your identity is confirmed.

For any questions or to expedite your request, please contact the Webmaster: Swaroop Patil at swarooppatil850@gmail.com.
